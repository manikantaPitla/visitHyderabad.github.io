Ramoji Film City

Ramoji Film City is the world's largest film studio complex, located on the outskirts of Hyderabad.

It was built in 1996 by Ramoji Rao, the founder of the Eenadu newspaper group. It was initially built as a film studio complex, but over the years, it has evolved into a tourist destination.

The sprawling complex spans over 2,000 acres and features a range of film sets, sound stages, production facilities, and other amenities required for filmmaking. It is one of the largest and most advanced film production centers in the world, with state-of-the-art equipment and facilities.

The complex was built with an investment of over 1 billion rupees and is considered one of the most expensive film production centers in the world.

Tourism in Ramoji Film City

Film studio tour: Ramoji Film City offers guided tours of its film studios, where you can see the behind-the-scenes action of a movie production. You can witness the making of a movie, visit the sets and locations, and get an idea of the film-making process.

Adventure rides: Ramoji Film City has a range of adventure rides and activities that you can enjoy, including roller coasters, zip-lining, and ATV rides.

Live shows: Ramoji Film City has several live shows that are entertaining and informative. These shows showcase different aspects of Indian culture, including dance performances, musical shows, and puppet shows.

Gardens and parks: The complex has several beautifully landscaped gardens and parks, including the Japanese garden, the Mughal garden, and the Sun Fountain Garden. These gardens provide a peaceful and serene environment for visitors to relax and enjoy the surroundings.

Exhibitions and museums: Ramoji Film City has several exhibitions and museums that showcase the history of Indian cinema, the making of a movie, and the evolution of technology in filmmaking.

Shopping and dining: The complex has several shops and restaurants where you can shop for souvenirs and enjoy a range of cuisines, including Indian, Chinese, and continental.

Accommodation: Ramoji Film City has several hotels and guest houses that offer a luxurious and comfortable stay to the visitors. These hotels are designed to provide a unique and immersive experience of the film industry.

Interesting about Ramoji Film City

  • Ramoji Film City has received several accolades over the years, including the Guinness World Record for the largest film studio complex in the world.

  • Ramoji Film City offers guided tours of its film studios, where you can see the behind-the-scenes action of a movie production. You can witness the making of a movie, visit the sets and locations, and get an idea of the film-making process.

  • Ramoji Film City is a popular destination for filmmakers, both from India and abroad. Many famous movies, including Baahubali, Dilwale, and Chennai Express, have been shot at the complex.

  • Ramoji Film City has received several awards and accolades for its contribution to the film industry and tourism. It has been recognized as the best tourism destination by the Government of India and has received the National Tourism Award several times
