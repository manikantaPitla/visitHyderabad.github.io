Laad Bazaar

Laad Bazaar is a bustling shopping district in Hyderabad that is famous for its bangles and other traditional jewelry. The narrow streets are lined with shops selling everything from traditional clothing to spices and sweets. Visitors can also try local street food and watch as skilled artisans create intricate jewelry by hand.

The market is located near the iconic Charminar monument and is easily accessible by public transport. The bazaar is a maze of narrow alleys and lanes, lined with small shops and stalls selling everything from bangles and jewelry to traditional clothing and souvenirs.

Laad Bazaar is particularly famous for its bangles, which are available in a wide range of colors and designs. The bangles are made by skilled artisans using traditional techniques and are considered to be some of the best in the world.

Apart from bangles, Laad Bazaar also offers a variety of other traditional handicrafts, including lacquer work, embroidery, and weaving. Visitors can also sample some of the local delicacies, including Irani chai and Osmania biscuits, which are sold by small food stalls in the market.

Laad Bazaar is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in Hyderabad's rich cultural heritage and traditional handicrafts. The market is open every day of the week and is a great place to shop for souvenirs and gifts to take back home.

Things available in Laad Bazaar

Bangles: The bangles come in a wide range of colors and designs, including gold and silver work, studded with precious and semi-precious stones, and featuring intricate patterns and motifs. The bangles are available in different sizes, ranging from small to extra-large, to fit every wrist.

Lacquer work: Laad Bazaar is famous for its lacquer work, which is used to create bangles, jewelry, and other decorative items. Lacquer work involves coating a base material with a layer of resin or varnish, which is then decorated with intricate designs and patterns.

Embroidery: Hyderabad is known for its exquisite embroidery work, which is done by skilled artisans using traditional techniques. You can find a variety of embroidered fabrics, including sarees, dupattas, and kurtas, in Laad Bazaar.

Pearls: Hyderabad is also known as the "Pearl City" because of its long association with pearl trading. Laad Bazaar offers a wide range of pearl jewelry, including necklaces, earrings, and bracelets.

Traditional clothing: Laad Bazaar is a great place to shop for traditional clothing, including sarees, salwar kameez, and kurtas. The clothing is available in a range of fabrics, including cotton, silk, and chiffon.

Handicrafts: You can find a variety of handicrafts in Laad Bazaar, including brassware, woodwork, and pottery. These handicrafts are made by skilled artisans using traditional techniques and make great souvenirs and gifts.
